Refund & Cancellation Policy

We value your trust and strive to provide the best shopping experience. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully.

1. Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • Once the order is processed or shipped, cancellation is not allowed.
  • Customized or stitched products cannot be cancelled once production has started.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are applicable only for damaged, defective, or incorrect products.
  • You must report the issue within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Refunds will be processed after verification of the issue.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Customized or made-to-order products.
  • Products purchased during sale or discount offers.
  • Used or damaged items not caused during delivery.

4. Refund Process

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Amount will be credited via the original payment method.
